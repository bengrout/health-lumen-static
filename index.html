Virtual populations. Real decisions.

We create virtual populations to help answer complex population health questions on the impact of interventions, for real world decision making.

HealthLumen's mission is to improve global health by using a sophisticated modelling platform to forecast trends in risk factors and chronic diseases, and simulating their impact on population health and the economy.

Solutions

Our solutions are based on a leading-edge micro-simulation model, developed over the past 12 years, which has the capability to model trends in risk factors and disease, and quantify the impact of policy options and interventions

Track record

Our micro-simulation model was developed over 12 years as part of the UK Health Forum's program in public health policy research and advocacy
