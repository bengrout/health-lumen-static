Team

Health Lumen has an experienced and multi-disciplinary team of software engineers, population health and policy research experts, and mathematicians.

Simon Lande, PhD
Co-Founder and CEO

Simon Lande is co-founder and CEO of HealthLumen. Prior to this, Simon was the CEO and founder of ActiveStandards, a pioneering digital governance and compliance software service, designed to help multinational corporations ensure quality and accuracy across their global web presence. ActiveStandards became the established market leader, resulting in a US private equity acquisition in November 2015.

Simon holds holds a BSc from Leeds University, and a PhD in Pharmacology from the Royal Free Hospital School of Medicine (University of London), which he followed with post-doctoral research on the neuromuscular junction, in the Department of Biochemistry at Imperial College, London.

Simon has a longstanding involvement with the National Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Children (NSPCC), including a year spent on the Executive Board (2017-2018) conducting a strategic review of income generation.
